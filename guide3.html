What Is Passive Income?

Passive income is income that continues without daily active work. You build something once and earn multiple times. Examples include ebooks, digital notes, templates and online guides.

1. Sell Study Notes As Ebook

Students already prepare notes for exams. Convert those notes into organized PDF format. Upload on CreatorHub marketplace. Each sale generates profit without rewriting content.

2. Create Skill Based Guides

If you know basic editing, coding, designing or marketing, create beginner guides. Many students search for simple step-by-step explanations. This demand creates earning potential.

3. Digital Templates

Resume templates, Instagram post templates, budget planners, study planners are high demand digital items. Design once and sell multiple times.

4. Online Micro Courses

Short PDF based mini courses are popular. Instead of long complicated courses, students prefer concise learning materials.

5. Affiliate Promotion

Promote useful tools or services. Earn commission when someone purchases through your link. Combine this with digital product selling.
