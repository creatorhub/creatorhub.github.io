CreatorHub Platform Rules

1. Platform Overview

CreatorHub is a digital marketplace that allows creators to sell ebooks and digital learning materials to buyers. The platform connects sellers and buyers while providing payment processing, ebook delivery, and wallet services.

2. Seller Rules

Sellers must upload only original or legally licensed ebooks. Uploading pirated, copied, or copyrighted material without permission is strictly prohibited. If copyright violation is detected, CreatorHub may remove the ebook and suspend the seller account permanently.

3. Commission Policy

CreatorHub automatically deducts 10% platform commission from every successful ebook sale. Example: If ebook price = ₹100 Seller receives ≈ ₹90 CreatorHub receives ≈ ₹10 For printed book orders, printing and delivery charges may also be deducted before seller earnings are calculated.

4. Printed Book Orders

If a buyer chooses a printed book option: • Printing cost is deducted from the order amount. • Delivery cost is added to the buyer payment. • The order is automatically sent to the printing service. Seller earnings are calculated after deducting printing cost and platform commission.

5. Wallet System

All earnings from ebook sales are automatically credited to the seller wallet. Wallet balance represents the total amount earned by the seller through product sales. The platform maintains a secure wallet system for tracking earnings and withdrawals.

6. Withdrawal Policy

Sellers can withdraw wallet balance to their UPI account. Withdrawal requests may go through automated security checks to prevent fraud. Processing time may vary depending on payment system verification.

7. Refund Policy

Digital products cannot be refunded once delivered. After an ebook is purchased and unlocked in the user library, the purchase is considered final. Refunds may only be issued in rare cases such as technical errors or duplicate payments.

8. Fraud Protection

CreatorHub uses automated monitoring systems to detect suspicious activities such as fake payments, unusual withdrawals, or abuse of the platform. Accounts involved in fraudulent activity may be suspended or permanently banned.

9. User Conduct

Users must not engage in illegal activities including: • Fraudulent payments • Hacking attempts • Unauthorized distribution of ebooks • Uploading harmful or illegal content Violation of these rules may lead to account suspension.

10. Platform Rights

CreatorHub reserves the right to modify rules, remove content, suspend accounts, or update policies when necessary to maintain platform security and compliance. By continuing to use CreatorHub services, users agree to follow all platform rules.
