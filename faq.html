What is CreatorHub?
CreatorHub is a digital marketplace where creators can sell ebooks and users can buy digital products safely.
How do I create an account?
Register on CreatorHub and verify your email to access all features.
How do I become a seller?
Go to dashboard → Become Seller → submit request → wait for approval.
How long does approval take?
Seller approval usually takes 12 to 24 hours.
How do buyers purchase ebooks?
Buyers click Buy Now and complete payment securely.
Refund available?
No refunds available because digital downloads cannot be reversed.
How contact support?
Use contact support form or AI support chat available on support page.
