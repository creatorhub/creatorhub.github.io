CreatorHub FAQ
What is CreatorHub?
CreatorHub is a digital marketplace where creators can sell ebooks and users can buy digital products safely.
How do I create an account?
Use the Register page and verify your email before accessing dashboard features.
How do I become a seller?
Apply using Become Seller option and wait for admin approval.
How long does approval take?
Seller approval normally takes between 12 to 24 hours.
How do buyers purchase ebooks?
Buyers simply click Buy Ebook and complete payment to access the digital file.
Where are purchased ebooks stored?
Purchased ebooks appear inside your personal My Library section.
Refund available?
No refunds are available because digital downloads cannot be reversed.
Seller earning percentage?
Sellers receive 90% earnings after admin commission.
Does CreatorHub allow copyrighted ebooks?
No. Copyright violations are strictly prohibited.
How contact support?
Use the support page contact form or AI support section.

Still have questions?

Our support team is here to help you anytime.
