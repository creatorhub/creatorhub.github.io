Refund Policy

CreatorHub is a digital marketplace where creators can sell ebooks and digital learning resources. This refund policy explains how purchases and refunds are handled on our platform.

1. Digital Product Policy

All products sold on CreatorHub are digital products such as ebooks and downloadable files. Once the purchase is completed and the product is delivered or made accessible to the buyer, the transaction is considered final.

2. No Refund Policy

Due to the nature of digital products, CreatorHub generally does not provide refunds, returns, or exchanges after a purchase has been successfully completed.

We strongly recommend reviewing the product description, preview, and details carefully before purchasing.

3. Exceptions

Refunds may be considered only in the following situations:

  • Payment was completed but the ebook was not delivered.
  • The downloaded file is corrupted or cannot be opened.
  • A duplicate payment was made due to a system error.

In such cases, our support team will review the issue and provide an appropriate resolution.

4. Technical Issues

If you face technical problems such as:

  • Unable to download the ebook
  • File not opening properly
  • Payment completed but product not delivered

Please contact our support team. We will investigate and resolve the issue as soon as possible.

5. Fraud & Abuse

CreatorHub reserves the right to suspend accounts, restrict access, or cancel services if fraudulent activity, chargeback abuse, or violations of our platform policies are detected.
