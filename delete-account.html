Delete Your Account

We’re sorry to see you leave CreatorHub. Before deleting your account, please read the information below carefully.

Important: Deleting your account will disable access immediately.

  • You will not be able to login after deletion.
  • Your marketplace data will be scheduled for removal.
  • Your account will be permanently deleted after 90 days.
  • You can recover your account anytime within those 90 days.

If you created ebooks, uploaded content, or made purchases, those records may remain temporarily for security and transaction verification.

CreatorHub keeps account information during the recovery period to allow users to restore access if the deletion request was accidental.

After 90 days, all personal information associated with your account will be permanently removed from our system and cannot be restored.
