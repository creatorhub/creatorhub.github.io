Advertising Policy

CreatorHub may display advertisements to support platform maintenance, hosting costs and content development. This page explains how advertisements, sponsored content and affiliate links are handled transparently.

Third-Party Advertising

We may use third-party advertising services such as Google AdSense. These networks may use cookies to show relevant advertisements based on user interests and browsing behavior.

Sponsored Content

If CreatorHub publishes sponsored content, it will always be clearly disclosed within the article. Transparency with users is a core principle of our platform.

Affiliate Links

Some articles may contain affiliate links. If users purchase through these links, CreatorHub may earn a small commission without additional cost to the user.

Ad Placement Standards

Advertisements will never interfere with user experience. CreatorHub avoids misleading ad placements, forced clicks or deceptive advertisement formatting.

User Privacy

Advertising partners may collect non-personal data according to their own privacy policies. Users are encouraged to review our Privacy Policy for further information.

Policy Updates

This advertising policy may be updated periodically to reflect regulatory requirements or advertising partner changes. Updates will always be reflected on this page.
